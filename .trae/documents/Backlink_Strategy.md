# FixRez AI Backlink Acquisition Strategy

## Overview
This document outlines a comprehensive backlink acquisition strategy to improve FixRez AI's domain authority and search engine rankings through quality external links.

## Target Backlink Profile
- **Goal**: Acquire 25-50 high-quality backlinks within 6 months
- **Quality over Quantity**: Focus on DA 30+ websites with relevant content
- **Relevance**: Prioritize career, job search, HR, and technology websites
- **Diversity**: Mix of different types of backlinks (guest posts, directories, partnerships, etc.)

## Quality Control Measures

### Link Quality Standards
- **Domain Authority**: Minimum DA 30 (prefer DA 50+)
- **Relevance Score**: Must be relevant to career/job search
- **Traffic**: Minimum 1,000 monthly organic visitors
- **Content Quality**: No spam, thin content, or link farms
- **Link Placement**: Contextual links in body content preferred

### Red Flags to Avoid
- Private blog networks (PBNs)
- Link farms or automated link building
- Irrelevant websites (casino, adult, pharmaceutical)
- Websites with manual Google penalties
- Sites with excessive outbound links
- Duplicate content websites

## Tracking & Measurement

### Key Metrics
1. **Domain Authority Growth**: Target 15+ point increase in 6 months
2. **Referring Domains**: Track new referring domains monthly
3. **Organic Traffic**: Monitor organic traffic growth
4. **Brand Mentions**: Track unlinked brand mentions
5. **Conversion Rate**: Measure traffic quality from backlinks

### Tools to Use
- Ahrefs for backlink monitoring
- Moz for domain authority tracking
- Google Search Console for performance data
- SEMrush for competitive analysis
- BuzzSumo for content performance

### Monthly Reporting
- New backlinks acquired
- Domain authority changes
- Traffic from referral sources
- Keyword ranking improvements
- Content performance metrics

## Risk Management

### Google Guidelines Compliance
- No paid links without proper disclosure
- Natural anchor text distribution
- Avoid exact-match anchor text overuse
- Focus on editorial links over manual placement
- Maintain natural link velocity

### Contingency Plans
- **Penalty Recovery**: Disavow file ready for toxic links
- **Algorithm Updates**: Monitor and adapt strategy
- **Competitor Analysis**: Regular competitive research
- **Industry Changes**: Stay updated on SEO best practices
